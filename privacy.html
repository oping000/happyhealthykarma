Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Happy Healthy Karma ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website happyhealthykarma.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information to provide and improve our service:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, search queries entered, and general interaction data collected automatically via Google Analytics.
  • Location Data: If you use the "Use my location" feature, your browser shares your approximate location with us temporarily to perform a nearby provider search. We do not store your location data.
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, and operating system collected automatically for analytics purposes.

We do not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal health information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide healthcare provider search results based on your condition and location
  • Improve and optimize our website performance
  • Understand how users interact with our service via anonymous analytics
  • Monitor and prevent abuse or technical issues

3. Affiliate Disclosure

⚠️ Important Disclosure

Happy Healthy Karma participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means we may earn a commission if you click on certain links on our website and make a purchase or sign up for a service — at no additional cost to you.

We partner with telehealth and mental health service providers including but not limited to Online-Therapy.com, Talkspace, and Calmerry. These partnerships help us keep Happy Healthy Karma free for all users.

We only recommend services we believe may be genuinely helpful to our users. Affiliate relationships do not influence our provider search results.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data about how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to track visits. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

6. Third-Party Services

Our service relies on the following third-party providers to function:

  • OpenStreetMap & Overpass API: Used to retrieve nearby healthcare provider data based on your search.
  • Nominatim: Used to convert location names to coordinates for searching.
  • Leaflet.js: Used to display the interactive map.
  • Google Fonts: Used to display website typography.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

7. Cookies

We use cookies for analytics and advertising purposes as described above. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. Children's Privacy

Happy Healthy Karma is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We do not store sensitive personal or health information.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data including the right to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
