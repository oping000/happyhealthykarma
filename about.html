About Happy Healthy Karma

Happy Healthy Karma was built with one simple belief — finding the right healthcare provider should not be complicated. When you are not feeling well, the last thing you should have to do is figure out which type of specialist you need.

We built a free app that lets you search by condition or symptom instead of specialty. Type what is wrong and we find the right care near you. No insurance knowledge needed. No signup required. Completely free.

We cover physical health, mental health, and alternative and holistic care including acupuncture, chiropractic, massage therapy, and naturopathic medicine. Telehealth options are also available for those who prefer care from home — no insurance needed, visits from $37.

Our Mission

Healthcare should be accessible to everyone regardless of insurance status, medical knowledge, or financial situation. Happy Healthy Karma exists to remove the barriers between people and the care they need.

What We Offer

Physical health provider search — find ENT specialists, cardiologists, orthopedic doctors, dermatologists, pediatricians, and more near you by searching your condition or symptom.

Mental health provider search — find therapists, psychiatrists, counselors, and ADHD specialists near you. Online therapy options also available 24/7.

Alternative and holistic care — find acupuncturists, chiropractors, massage therapists, and naturopathic doctors near you.

Telehealth — see a doctor online from $37, no insurance needed, no appointment required.

Free Forever

Happy Healthy Karma is and always will be free for users. We are supported by affiliate partnerships with telehealth and wellness providers. We only recommend services we believe are genuinely helpful.
